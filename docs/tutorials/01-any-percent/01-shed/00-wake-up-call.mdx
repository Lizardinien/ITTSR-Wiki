## Extra Details
These are irrelevant for most players but worth mentioning.
You can chapterselect Intro and reset your timer manully. Your timer will then start in the loading to Wake-up Call and you'll be able to buffer the cutscene. Buffering the cutscene saves more time the worse the ping is up to half a second. You have to disable automatic resets in your Livesplit for this to work properly.
If you in the air before you chapterselect you can get a timed dash once loading in. This can be redone as May before the rcp doing the fuse glitch.
Cody can jump out of the lauch, that the fuse pick up gives, making you land quickier and saving less than a second.
