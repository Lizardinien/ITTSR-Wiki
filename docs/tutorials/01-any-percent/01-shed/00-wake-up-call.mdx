## Some tiny extra details
You can buffer the Wake-up Call intro cutscene by chapterselecting Intro and reseting your timer manully. Your timer will then start in the loading to Wake-up Call and you'll be able to buffer the cutscene. Not buffering the cutscene losses more time the worse the ping is up to half a second. You have to disable automatic resets in your Livesplit for this to work properly.

If you're in the air before you chapterselect you can get a timed dash once loading in. This can be redone as May before the rcp doing the fuse glitch.

Cody can jump out of the lauch, that the fuse pick up gives, making you fall and put it in cleaner.
