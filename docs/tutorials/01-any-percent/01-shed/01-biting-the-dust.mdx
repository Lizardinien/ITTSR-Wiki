## Boss fight

If you rcped to get the second player up in the previous section you should be on 30 FPS already. If you were together, you can rcp the moment you load into the actual fight to then switch to 30 FPS.

#### Bomb phases

Every bomb phase the boss is gonna shot 5 Bombs at each player. Then you can suck them up and fire them back at the boss. If you don't lose any bombs and do this phase twice you'll bring the boss down to zero hp and will proceed to the finnishing phase.

:::easy Easy alternative
Place all bombs on one side of the arena without stacking them. Crouch walking gives a pretty good spacing.
![](img/btd_boss_bomb_area)
:::

:::hard 
If you place all bombs very tightly together in a line along the area that would suck up the bombs without you touching the tube, you can just bumb into the side of the tube and move it that way saving ?.
:::
